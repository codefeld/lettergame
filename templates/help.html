Each game begins with a five-letter mystery word that you need to guess. Before you guess the word, you will need to get clues from your friends.

To get clues, share the mystery word with your friends. When you click the "share" button, it will a message and a link that's unique to your mystery word. Post the message in a Slack channel, text message, or on social media. When your friends click the link, they'll be able to create clues from your mystery word.

The clues will contain letters from your mystery word which you will see as blue numbers. They will also contain letters that are not in your word, which you will see as yellow letters. For example, if your mystery word is LABEL, and your friend created the clue SLICE, you would see:

The S, I, and C in the clue are yellow because they are not letters in your mystery word. The L is replaced by a blue 1 (but your friend could have used 5 instead) and the E is replaced by a blue 4 because of its position in the mystery word.
